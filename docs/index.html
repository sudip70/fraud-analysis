Key Insights
🌍
International + New Merchant
The "Both" combination carries the highest fraud rate — the single strongest combined signal in the dataset.
🕐
Late-Night Spike
Fraud peaks after 22:00. Unusual_Time_Transaction ranks in the top 3 predictors across all models.
📍
Distance from Home
Fraudulent transactions occur on average 2× farther from home — a strong standalone risk indicator.
📈
Prior Fraud = #1 Predictor
Even a single prior fraud incident dramatically increases probability. Recidivism is the strongest individual feature.
